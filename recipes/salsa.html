Salsa

Description

This simple spicy salsa dish will go perfectly with a bag of Tostidos. Created with the freshest veggies, this salsa is an all time favorite among thousands of people.

Ingredients

  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lemons
  • Bell Peppers
  • Salt
  • Green Chilis
  • Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Dice all veggies
  2. Mix canned tomatoes and veggies in a big bowl
  3. Use an immersion blender to blend together the tomatoes and veggies
  4. Add lemon juice, salt, and chili flakes to taste

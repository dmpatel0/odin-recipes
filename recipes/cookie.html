Cookies

Description

These delicious cookies are perfect for a midnight snack, or perhaps as a treat during halloween!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cocao Powder
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix everything together to create the dough
  3. Bake in the oven until the cookeis are done

Smoothie

Description

This smoothie is the perfect treat after a long days work outside Packed with fruits, it not only tastes good, but also provides numerous anti-oxidants!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Mixed frozen berries
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla ice-cream

Steps

  1. Pour the milk and the frozen berries in to the blender
  2. Blend until the mixture has a thick pastey consistency
  3. Add in the sugar, and continue blending until it becomes thinner
  4. Pour into a glass and top with ice cream before serving
